Sofia the first

Sofia the First tells the story of a village girl who becomes a princess when her mother, Miranda,
marries King Roland of Enchancia. The stories focus on Sofia's adventures learning to be a princess,
adapting to her new family, and using the magical Amulet of Avalor to help others. A central theme is
that true royalty comes from the heart, as Sofia learns to balance her commoner background with her new royal life.

Sofia the First is a story about a young village girl, Sofia, whose life changes forever when her mother, Miranda,
marries King Roland of Enchancia. Sofia becomes a princess, moves into a castle, and must navigate her new royal life and her relationship with her step-siblings, Amber and James. King Roland gives her the magical Amulet
of Avalor, which allows her to communicate with animals and connects her to other princesses who can help her. The story follows Sofia as she learns the true meaning of being a princess through adventures that test her kindness and heart, while the sorcerer Cedric repeatedly tries to steal the Amulet's power.
